About

Formerly the IEEE Neural Network Council, the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society focuses on the theory, design, application, and development of nature- and brain-inspired knowledge representation and computational paradigms together with learning and artificially intelligent systems in which these paradigms are contained.

IEEE CIS emphasizes the study and application of: Neural networks, Evolutionary algorithms, Swarm intelligence algorithms, Fuzzy and rule-based systems, and Cognitive systems. Furthermore, Computational Intelligence (CI), often another name for Artificial Intelligence, is the theory, design, application and development of biologically and linguistically motivated computational paradigms. Traditionally the three main pillars of CI have been Neural Networks, Fuzzy Systems, and Evolutionary Computation.
